Senco is one stop solution for all your doubts and queries. It provide one to one live interaction with mentors. By doubts it means both academic and non-academic.

Senco is a unique startup that gives a complete hand-holding experience to the students, every step of the way. Mentors help students in complete development and guide as both a friend and an expert. Below are some of the key highlights of how it can help you.

Planner - Create weekly goals for your study routine and track how you are improving! A Free offering by from our side.

Chat with Coach - Once you subscribe, you can connect with your coach in an instant!

Live Session with Coach - Upon subscribing, connect with your coach, virtually and LIVE!
